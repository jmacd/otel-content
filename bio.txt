Josh MacDonald is a Software Engineer at LightStep and a member of the
OpenTelemetry technical committee.

Josh has been working on observability systems for the last 15 years,
having worked on monitoring, logging, tracing, and metrics systems as
well as logging and tracing APIs at Google.  Previously, he created
Xdelta, an open-source compression tool.  Currently, he is focused on
sampling algorithms and data engineering at LightStep.
